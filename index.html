A community of nature enthusiasts

The Nature Club of IISc is a vibrant community of nature lovers inhabiting the beautiful campus of the Institute. Situated amidst the vibrant urban sprawl of modern-day Bangalore, the biodiverse campus of the Institute is a thriving habitat of its own.

Home to a wide variety of trees — both native to Bangalore and hailing from the Western Ghats — the IISc campus offers a semi-forest landscape that changes beautifully with the seasons. Each month brings a new celebration: trees burst into bloom and shower the campus with vibrant colors and tasty fruits (Ahh, but please ID the tree correctly before taking a bite! XD)

We're always up to something—from night walks to weekend treks. Check out what we did this past year.
